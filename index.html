How It Works

Our simulation uses two key modalities to estimate depression levels.

Textual Sentiment Analysis

By analyzing the words you use to express your feelings, the system searches for patterns commonly associated with different mood states. Words expressing fatigue, sadness, or isolation impact the analysis.

Facial Expression Analysis

Facial analysis algorithms detect micro-expressions and general facial features to classify emotions into categories like neutral, happy, sad, or angry.

Technologies Used

HTML5, CSS3, Vanilla JavaScript, and Chart.js. Note: This is a frontend demonstration simulating backend ML predictions.
